RequiredAspect

(JVM) @Target([AnnotationTarget.CLASS]) annotation class RequiredAspect

Only applicable to subclasses of Aspect.

This annotation indicates that an Aspect is required and must always be loaded. It should be used sparingly.

Required Aspects will be loaded before non-required Aspects.

Parameters

priority - The priority for loading in relation to other required Aspects. Higher priorities load first.
